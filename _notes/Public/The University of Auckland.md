<h4 id="Environmental-change">1.1.1 <a href="https://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/science/about-the-faculty/school-of-environment/environment-research/environmental-change.html" target="_blank">Environmental changeï¼Œç¯å¢ƒå˜åŒ–</a></h4>
<p><strong>ä»‹ç»ï¼š</strong>We research the origin, evolution, and adaptation of life on Earth, the adaptation of tropical islands to climate change, and the use of proxy records for paleoenvironmental change.
